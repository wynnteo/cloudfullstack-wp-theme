About Me

Hello, I'm Erik

I'm a full-stack developer based in Stockholm, Sweden, with a passion for creating beautiful, functional, and user-centered digital experiences. My approach to development is heavily influenced by Scandinavian design principles: simplicity, functionality, and attention to detail.

With over 8 years of experience in web development, I've worked with everything from small startups to large enterprises, always focusing on writing clean, maintainable code and creating solutions that truly serve users' needs.
